Rules inside the doomed world

Inside Ignis Inanis, the laws of nature are twisted by the relentless agony that permeates the very fabric of this forsaken realm. Here, survival hinges on understanding and adapting to the brutal rules that govern existence amidst the chaos.

1. Eternal conflict: the life in this world is beyond any hope for peace or compassion. Only the strongest survive, and alliances are fleeting, forged in necessity and shattered by betrayal. The insatiable hunger for power almost destroys any chance of progress or survival, so the demoniac inhabitants create a society where only the strongest enough could rule the weak.

2. Fire and blood: fire is both a weapon and a symbol of the world's torment. It consumes everything in its path, leaving behind ashes and despair. Those foolish enough to lose the control of the flames are condened be reduced to nothing more than scum, losing all they had and becoming even lower in the social scale than the slaves.

3. Hierarchy build on powers: social structure is rigid and unforgiving, with power concentrated in the hands of those who can inflict the most suffering. The weak are subjugated, and their pain fuels the ambitions of the merciless elite.
